About Me

Background

  • Born and raised in Boston, Massachusetts, U.S.A.
  • Serving on the Holy Altar since 2022
  • Candidate for Priesthood in the Armenian Prelacy of the Eastern U.S.

Formal Education

  • 4th Year Computer Science Student at the University of Massachusetts Boston (2022-2026)
  • Waltham Sr. High School (2018-2022)
  • St. Stephen's Armenian Saturday School (2008-2022)

Personal Mission

  • To share and revitalize the sacred traditions of the Armenian Church
  • To educate our laypeople about our Holy Church
  • To educate myself as much as possible in our Church's traditions, liturgics, and theology

What's Next?

  • Once I receive my Bachelor's degree, I hope to go to the Holy See of Cilicia's Armenian Theological Seminary (Tbrevank) for formal theological education.
